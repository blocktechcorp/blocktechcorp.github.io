Welcome

Dec 14, 2015

Hello World! We are very excited to welcome you to Enter the Blockchain, a blog and informational resource for the community produced by Blockchain Tech Corp.

We believe the blockchain will change the world and our goal is to create high quality, free, educational information to help everyone understand why and how they can help.

What’s Inside:

A blog: We will be posting periodically about developments in blockchain technology, Bitcoin, cryptography, open source software, and the latest news from Blockchain Tech Corp.

Information: Informational resources to learn more about the amazing technologies, concepts, and potential in this exciting new space.

Guides: Step-by-step guides designed to help you get up and running with Bitcoin and blockchain technology.

Check back regularly or subscribe to our RSS feed to make sure you don’t miss anything!
