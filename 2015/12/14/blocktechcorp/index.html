Meet Blockchain Technologies Corp.

Dec 14, 2015

Find Us: Web | Twitter | Facebook | CrunchBase | AngelList

We Put The Blockchain To Work.

Blockchain Tech Corp. is an innovative software development company creating unique solutions to real-world problems by leveraging the power of blockchain technology.

Our blockchain voting system uses verifiable blockchain audit trails to restore trust in democratic elections by minimizing the potential for foul play.

We also operate cutting edge Bitcoin ATMs in New York City that allow anyone to buy into Bitcoin with cash on demand, lowering the barrier to entry for those that want to get started using Bitcoin.

We Are 100% Dedicated To Bitcoin & The Blockchain.

Our founder, Nick Spanos, has been involved with Bitcoin since the early days.

In late 2013, Nick opened Bitcoin Center NYC, a large 5,000 sq. ft. retail space right on Wall Street - directly across from the New York Stock Exchange.
